#+TITLE: Darcy's Law

#+BEGIN_EXPORT latex
Q = -\frac{k}{\mu} (\nabla P - \rho g)
#+END_EXPORT
- Darcy velocity, Q, is the volume per unit time flowing through the cross section
- actual velocity, v, is given as
#+BEGIN_EXPORT latex
v = \frac{Q}{\phi} = -\frac{k}{\mu} (\nabla P - \rho g)
#+END_EXPORT
